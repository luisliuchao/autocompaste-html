Instructions

Dear participant , please read the instructions before the experiment.

We are doing this experiment to test our new copy-paste techinique AUTOCOMPASTE compared to the traditional ctrl + c, ctrl + v technique.

After clicking 'Next', you will see the screen as below.

When the Technique is TRADITIONAL, find the corresponding text in the red box from the Article Windowns, use ctrl + c, ctrl + v to copy and paste to the Text Editor window.

When the Technique is AUTOCOMPASTE, directly type the to-be-copied text in the Text Editor window and find the correponding matching text from the dropdown list.

When Highlighted is Yes, the to-be-copied text will be marked with yellow background in one of the Article windows.

When Highlighted is No, the to-be-copied text will not be marked with yellow background.

Once done the pasting the text, click 'Next Task' to the next round.

There will be total 1 rounds and each round consists of 12 trials.
